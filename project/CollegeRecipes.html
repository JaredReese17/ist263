I found my love of cooking at the unfortunate expense of one of my brother’s health. My brother, Aidan, was diagnosed with celiac disease last year. This turned the culture of my Italian household upside down. No more giant pasta meals. “Sunday sauce” became analytical to make sure there was no traces of gluten inside. Since his diagnosis, he started to cook every day, and teaching me some of his favorite meals to make as well. From there, my love of cooking took off. This year at school, I finally have my own kitchen. After three years of either dorm dining halls of fraternity house chefs, I am finally able to cook whatever I want, whenever I want (within reason, of course). I wanted to create this page to share some of my favorite meals with other students who are finally able to cook on their own. The recipes I have found to love are easy and incredibly adaptable for everyone’s taste.
